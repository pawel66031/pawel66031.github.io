Lamp Generator

A procedural tool in Geometry Nodes.

A tool created in Blender during a broadcast on Discord, demonstrating the capabilities of Geometry Nodes. It allows you to dynamically edit the parameters of the lamp, such as height, radius or scale of individual elements. The model has the correct topology ready for games, but does not include UV.

Blender file: lamp_generator.blend
