About Me

I'm a programmer and 3D graphic designer, specializing in working as a Technical Artist. My passion is exploring technologies related to real-time image processing and creating innovative procedural solutions. I love exploring new tools and methods that help in effective creative work.

Outside of work, I am involved in volunteer work, by mapping data at OpenStreetMap. I'm also an open source software enthusiast, which inspires me to share knowledge and support the community.

In my free time, I ride my bike, explore unknown corners and help 3D artists solve design problems and explain how tools work. I'm developing an independent game with colleagues, exploring the world of gamedev and discovering the secrets of game development.
